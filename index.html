About

From nourishing serums to breathable fabrics, Sundorica empowers every woman to feel beautiful — inside and out. Rooted in purity, luxury, and purpose, we believe beauty is not just what you wear, but how you care for yourself.

Privacy Policy

At Sundorica, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our brand through our Facebook page, online platforms, and during the purchase of skincare or clothing products.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery/shipping address
  • Product preferences
  • Your messages via Messenger, comments, or email

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Communicate with you about your purchases
  • Provide customer service
  • Send you order updates or promotional offers (if you agree)
  • Improve our products and services

3. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell your personal information to any third parties. Your data is only used by our internal team for order fulfillment and communication.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. All information is kept confidential and used only for business purposes.

5. Your Rights

You may request:

  • To view the data we hold about you
  • To correct or delete your personal information
  • To opt out of promotional messages at any time

We value your trust and are committed to handling your personal information responsibly.
